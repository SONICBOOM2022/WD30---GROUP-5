Frequently Asked Questions

Q. How old do i need to be to rent a Car from G' Five?

A. You can rent an G' Five Self Drive car if you are 18 years or above.

Q. How can I reserve a car?

A. You can make a reservation through our website www.gfive.com or mobile App.

Q. is there an option to rent a car for a longer period?

A. With G' Five Flex, allows you to get a price benefit when you book for 7 days and above on selected car models.

Q. I need to drop of the car at a different location, can I do that?

A. No. The car will be collected by our driver from the place to where it was delivered.

Q. What if I return the car late?

A. Guest needs to inform G' Five in advance for rental extension. An extension or grace period of up tp 1 hour is permissible if informed with no extra charges.
